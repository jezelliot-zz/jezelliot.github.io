The Project

Flexport has transformed global trade with a digital platform that provides the customer with full transparency and control. Tasked with positioning Flexport as the leader in freight-forwarding - as Creative Director, I lead the re-design of Flexport.com, from Strategy & Research, to UX and through Visual Design.

Flexport.com now more broadly communicates the vision and value proposition for the Operating System for Global Trade. It boasts more robust information about Flexport's ocean, air and trucking logistics services. And it more prominently features the most important strength as an organization: the people — with a far more expansive Careers section to support company culture and recruiting efforts.
