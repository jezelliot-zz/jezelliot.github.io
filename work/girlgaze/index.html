The Project

Girlgaze is a global network of over 250,000 female creatives that exists to close the gender gap — via an innovative jobs platform where the world's most best companies hire the best female and non-binary creative talent.

Working directly with Girlgaze, we (Little Troop) re-designed and launched v2 of the Girlgaze jobs platform along with an accompanying marketing site that highlights the benefits to Creators and Companies and brings the Girlgaze mission to life.
