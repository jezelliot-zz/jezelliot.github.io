The Project

Debuting in 2018, Ita Nia is a womens fashion label designed and made end-to-end in Dili, Timor-Leste.

Tasked with bringing their brand and first ever collection to the world, we launched a minimal and timeless ecommerce experience that lets beautiful photography hero the quality fit, fabric and craftsmanship of the label. The site looks to tease out the brands key value — minimalism & structure — and it's origin, Timor Leste.
