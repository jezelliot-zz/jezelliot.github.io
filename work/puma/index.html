The Project

The re-designed Puma.com now acts as a platform for rich brand content — while seamlessly integrating a best-in-class e-commerce experience.

As lead visual designer on the engagement with Puma's global e-commerce and marketing teams, the task was to imagine a solution that unified two existing experiences (one brand, one ecom) into one. The complete shopflow was re-designed using a component based modular approach providing flexibility and scalability across a deep product offering.
